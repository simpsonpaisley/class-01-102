10 Facts about Palm Trees!

  1. Palm trees are very tall!
  2. There are over 2,000 species of Palm Tree
  3. Palm trees are from the plant family Arecaeae.
  4. Palm trees can live in deserts, rainforests, on beaches, and many other places!
  5. Palm trees are used for decoration, food, shade, and more!
  6. Palm Trees area symbol of life
  7. Different types of palm trees produce different products, from coconut products, oils, dates, palm syrup, ivory nuts, carnauba wax, rattan cane, raffia, and palm wood.
